How can you make the best green juice at home?

People claim juicing is too expensive, but we've provided a table of three different examples of juices and their low costs per serving

Don't make it too complicated!!

Fruit Vegetable Total Price
Apple Cucumber $0.75
Lemon Carrot $0.60
Beet Celery $0.85

Here are some steps we'd suggest:

  1. Buy yourself a juicer

    2. This is a crucial step to the process
    You can't use a regular blender and expect it to come out like a juice

  2. Pick out a couple fruits & vegetables you think you'd like together

    3. Everyone is going to have different preferences.
    Our personal favorite green juice juice includes celery, cucumber, lemon & apple

  3. Wash your fruits and vegetables and peel if needed

    4. Get your vegetables all clean
    Some food with an acidic or bitter peel (like lemon) you will want to peel

  4. Slowly, one by one, add in your fruits and vegetables and watch them combine into your perfect juice

    5. Enjoy what you made and take notes for next time!!
